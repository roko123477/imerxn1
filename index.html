What is IMERXN

What is IMERXN

A resource platform that enables real-world experiences to support high school and college students to become career aware and career ready.

We ensure you make the right career choices while building relevant skills through applied learning in professional environments and community settings. These experiences allow you to gain practical skills in the field of study that you are exploring as a future career option. Our programs are a great booster to your profile, adding value to your resume and helping you stand out in a crowd.

How does IMERXN work


Mode
Students can participate in these programs either virtually or physically depending on the format of the program.

Duration
Every program is curated so each student gets the benefit of a highly personalised program structured based on individual needs and varied duration of 2-6 months depending on the program design.

Experience
The IMERXN team curates customized and great collective experiences by enabling collaborative communication between mentors, students and partners.
